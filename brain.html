Understanding Brain Health

Role of the Brain in the Body

The brain is the control center for all bodily functions, processing sensory information, coordinating movements, and influencing emotions. Maintaining brain health is crucial for overall well-being.

Common Causes of Brain Problems

  • Trauma or injury
  • Stroke
  • Neurodegenerative diseases
  • Genetic factors
  • Chronic stress

Symptoms to Watch For

  • Severe headaches
  • Memory loss or confusion
  • Seizures
  • Speech or vision problems
  • Loss of coordination

Remedies and Treatments

Treatment varies based on the condition but may include surgery, medications, rehabilitation, or lifestyle changes.
