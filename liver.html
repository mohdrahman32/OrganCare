Understanding Liver Health

Role of the Liver in the Body

The liver is essential for detoxifying the body, metabolizing nutrients, and producing vital proteins. Protecting liver health is crucial for overall well-being.

Common Causes of Liver Problems

  • Excessive alcohol consumption
  • Viral infections (hepatitis)
  • Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease
  • Obesity and poor diet
  • Genetic liver disorders

Symptoms to Watch For

  • Jaundice (yellowing of the skin or eyes)
  • Abdominal pain or swelling
  • Fatigue
  • Dark urine or light-colored stool
  • Loss of appetite

Remedies and Treatments

Treatment options include medication, lifestyle changes, and, in advanced cases, liver transplantation. Timely care is essential for the best outcomes.
