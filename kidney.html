Understanding Kidney Health

Role of Kidneys in the Body

Kidneys are vital organs responsible for filtering waste, balancing body fluids, and regulating blood pressure. Maintaining kidney health is essential for overall well-being.

Common Causes of Kidney Problems

  • Infections such as UTIs
  • Chronic conditions like diabetes and hypertension
  • Kidney stones
  • Genetic factors
  • Overuse of painkillers

Symptoms to Watch For

  • Swelling in legs and ankles
  • Fatigue and lack of energy
  • Frequent or painful urination
  • Blood in urine
  • High blood pressure

Remedies and Treatments

Timely diagnosis and treatment are crucial. Remedies include medication, lifestyle changes, and in severe cases, dialysis or kidney transplants.
