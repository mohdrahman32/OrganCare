Understanding Lung Health

Role of the Lungs in the Body

The lungs are essential for oxygenating the body and expelling carbon dioxide. Keeping the lungs healthy is crucial for overall well-being.

Common Causes of Lung Problems

  • Smoking and exposure to second-hand smoke
  • Air pollution and environmental toxins
  • Chronic diseases like asthma and COPD
  • Infections like pneumonia and tuberculosis

Symptoms to Watch For

  • Persistent cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or tightness
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Wheezing or noisy breathing

Remedies and Treatments

Treatment for lung problems includes medications, lifestyle changes, respiratory therapy, and, in severe cases, lung transplantation. Early diagnosis and care are essential for managing lung health effectively.
