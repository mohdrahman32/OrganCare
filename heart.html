Understanding Heart Health

Role of the Heart in the Body

The heart is a vital organ responsible for pumping blood throughout the body, supplying oxygen and nutrients, and removing waste products. Maintaining a healthy heart is essential for overall well-being.

Common Causes of Heart Problems

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Obesity and sedentary lifestyle
  • Smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Genetic predispositions

Symptoms to Watch For

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue and dizziness
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Swelling in the legs, ankles, or feet

Remedies and Treatments

Timely diagnosis and treatment are crucial. Remedies include medication, dietary changes, exercise, and in severe cases, surgeries such as angioplasty or bypass surgery.
